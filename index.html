Peaceful, Connected & Distinctly Ojai

A Valley Built for Living Well

Welcome to 11905 Silver Spur St., a beautifully updated Ojai home that captures the ease and charm of daily life in the valley. Open, light-filled spaces feel warm and inviting from the moment you enter, while the porch offers the perfect place to unwind with a glass of wine as the Topa Topas glow in Ojai's famed pink moment at sunset.

Set in a quiet location with convenient access to Santa Barbara and Ventura, the home is just a short stroll to the Ojai Trolley for brunch at the Ojai Valley Inn or an afternoon in downtown Ojai, where live music and local theater can round out the day.

Nearby are local eateries, a yoga studio, hiking trails, and the bike path leading toward the coast. It is a home that makes everyday life feel peaceful, connected, and distinctly Ojai.
